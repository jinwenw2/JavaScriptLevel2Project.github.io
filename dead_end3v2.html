You Dash Down the Stairs

Creak!

The stairs are shaking.

They cannot sustain your entire weight while running for long.

Creak! Creak!

You run with all your might but it is no use.

Snap! Crash!

The stairs give and you fall with them.

Crushed under a pile of debris, you hear a crash from the wall and glass shattering. A loud boom is heard from the outside.

You start losing feeling in your body. You realize the debris must have crushed it as your vision darkens.


Dead End

Running may not have been the best idea. The stairs cannot sustain your weight while running.
